Heat Vulnerability and Cooling Center Hotspot

This interactive map explores spatial patterns of heat vulnerability and potential access to cooling resources. The visualization highlights geographic areas where residents may face greater risks during extreme heat events due to demographic, socioeconomic, or environmental factors. By interacting with the map, users can hover over areas to see additional information about local conditions and indicators related to heat exposure and community vulnerability. Clicking on a location reveals more detailed statistics that help contextualize which populations may be most affected by extreme heat. Understanding where vulnerable populations live relative to cooling infrastructure can help planners and public agencies identify neighborhoods that may benefit from additional cooling centers, outreach, or climate adaptation strategies.
